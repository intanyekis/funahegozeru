Who Is The Richest In 2021?

Musician John Mayer rose to fame in the early 2000s with hits like "Your Body Is a Wonderland" and "Waiting on the World to Change". Since then, the singer released eight successful studio albums, and today he has seven Grammy awards in his home.

Considering that John Mayer often writes about his exes, one can't help but wonder just how well they are doing today. If you're wondering just which one of the singer's exes is the richest today — keep on scrolling to find out!

10 Scheana Shay Has A Net Worth Of $500 Thousand

Kicking the list off is Scheana Shay best known for starring in the Bravo reality television show Vanderpump Rules. Scheana Shay and John Mayer were linked to each other in April 2009 whoever their relationship didn't last long. According to Celebrity Net Worth, Scheana Shay is currently estimated to have a net worth of $500,000.

9 Minka Kelly Has A Net Worth Of $5 Million

Next on the list is actress Minka Kelly best known for starring in projects like Friday Night Lights, Parenthood, and Almost Human. Minka Kelly and John Mayer were linked to each other from September 2007 until January 2008. According to Celebrity Net Worth, the actress is currently estimated to have a net worth of $5 million.

8 Vanessa Carlton Has A Net Worth Of $10 Million

Let's move on to musician Vanessa Carlton who dated John Mayer from March 2002 until March 2003. The singer is best known for her hit "A Thousand Miles" which was released in 2002.

7 Rhona Mitra Has A Net Worth Of $10 Million

6 Jennifer Love Hewitt Has A Net Worth Of $22 Million

Another actress that made it onto today's list is Jennifer Love Hewitt best known for starring in movies like I Know What You Did Last Summer, Can't Hardly Wait, and Heartbreakers. 5 Jessica Simpson Has A Net Worth Of $200 Million

3 Katy Perry Has A Net Worth Of $330 Million
