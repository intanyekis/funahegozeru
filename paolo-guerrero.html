Biography

Paolo Guerrero was born in Lima, Peru on January 1, 1984. Peruvian striker who has been with Flamengo since 2015. He led Copa America in scoring in 2011 while playing for Hamburger SV. He also played for Corinthians from 2012 until 2015. He debuted for the Peruvian national team in 2004. He was teammates with Philipp Lahm on Bayern Munich from 2004 to 2006. He entered the Alianza Lima youth system at age 8.
