Four new tenants open at Gibson Mill Market WSOC TV

CONCORD, N.C. — Gibson Mill Market food hall in Concord has welcomed four new tenants.

Scratch Made Hospitality Group concepts Kuni’s Kitchen, Barco’s Sports & Raw Bar Churn Buddies and The Arcade are now open.

“Gibson Mill has been a local treasure for more than 100 years and we are grateful to join the market and create a variety of menu offerings for friends old and new to enjoy,” said Scratch Made Hospitality Group owner Ryan Bybee in a new release.

Kuni’s Kitchen offers scratch-made noodles and dumplings, fried rice, and salads. Kuni’s is open from 11 a.m. to 9 p.m. Wednesday through Saturday and 12 p.m. to 5 p.m. Sunday.

Barcos Sports & Raw Bar offers fresh seafood and sports-bar-friendly fare paired with beer, wine, and boat-inspired cocktails. Barcos is open from 3 p.m. to 9 p.m. Wednesday through Friday, 11 a.m. to 9 p.m. Saturday, and 12 p.m. to 5 p.m. Sunday.

Churn Buddies Ice Cream offers scratch-made ice cream featuring local ingredients and unique flavors. Churn Buddies is open from 11 a.m. to 2 p.m. and 4 p.m. to 9 p.m. Wednesday through Friday, 12 p.m. to 7:30 p.m. Saturday, and 12 p.m. to 5 p.m. Sunday.

The Arcade is open during regular market hours.

The new tenants join the market’s current roster, which includes Cara’s Cookie Company, Taco Street, Johnny Rogers BBQ & Burgers, Defined Coffee, and High Branch Brewing Company.

Representatives with Gibson Mill Market say there will be more vendor announcements soon, including updates and timing for Livy’s Neapolitan Pizza.

For more information, go to gibsonmill.com/market.
