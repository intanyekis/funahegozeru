Lil Wayne's New Girlfriend Confirms Their Relationship 1 Month after His Split from Rumored Fiance

Lil Wayne appears to have moved on since the split from his rumored fiancée, La'tecia Thomas, as he is sighted with another woman in a recent social media upload.

Just a month after Lil Wayne reportedly called it quits with Australian model, La'tecia Thomas, the famous, has been linked to another lady - model Denise Bidot, according to a People report.

Lil Wayne performs at the Bud Light Super Bowl Music Fest on January 30, 2020 | Photo: Getty Images

The 34-year-old model had gotten fans talking after she shared a collage of pictures that featured her and the “Lollipop” rapper engaged in a romantic embrace via her Instagram Story.

During the latest episode of his “Young Money Radio with Lil Wayne” series, the 37-year-old rapper hinted at having a woman in his life.

While one picture showed the rapper’s arms wrapped around his new beau, another image featured them sharing a kiss. A third image showed the duo smiling at the camera in the Story which was captioned:

“Somehow in the middle of all the madness, something special happened. Us.”

A Screenshot of Denise Bidot's Post on her Instagram Story | Photo: instagram.com/denisebidot/

Rap-Up reported that fans previously made the connection between the pair after they noticed that Denise is the only one that Wayne, real name Dwayne Michael Carter Jr., follows on Instagram.

Fans had also noticed that while promoting his Young Money collection with fashion brand, American Eagle, in 2019, Wayne was pictured next to Denise.

Daily Mail revealed that Denise is one of the faces that is associated with Rihanna's Savage X Fenty lingerie line. The plus-size model was most recently photographed during the lingerie line’s spring campaign.

The news outlet narrated that during the latest episode of his “Young Money Radio with Lil Wayne” series, the 37-year-old rapper hinted at having a woman in his life as he and Nicki Minaj talked about married life.

As regards his former rumored relationship with La'tecia, Cheat Sheet reported that sources claimed that the pair met in summer 2019.

One of her friends allegedly added that the “A Milli” rapper and the model fell in love with each other from the first time they met.

While neither party publicly admitted that they were an item, La'tecia was said to have posted photos in what seemed to be Wayne’s house and left flirty messages under his social media posts.

The model was subsequently seen rocking a massive ring that some people claim she began wearing in October 2019.

Wayne was previously married to Toya Wright, childhood sweetheart, with whom he shares Reginae Carter.
