Meet Riley Keough, born on May 29, 1989, in Santa Monica, California. She is the daughter of the renowned singer-songwriter Lisa Marie Presley and musician Danny Keough. Riley hails from a famous lineage as the granddaughter of the legendary Elvis Presley and celebrated actress Priscilla Presley.

Aside from her illustrious lineage, Riley has two half-sisters from her mother’s fourth marriage, forming a unique and dynamic family unit. In 2015, she tied the knot with Australian stuntman Ben Smith-Petersen, exemplifying love that transcends borders. The couple welcomed a beautiful daughter into their lives, creating a bond that encompasses both adventure and devotion.

In the midst of her thriving career, Riley Keough embraces the importance of family, love, and the joy of motherhood.
