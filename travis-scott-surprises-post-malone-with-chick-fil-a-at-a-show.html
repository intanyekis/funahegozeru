Travis Scott Surprises Post Malone With Chick-fil-A at a Show

Travis Scott is one good friend! The “Butterfly Effect” rapper surprised pal Post Malone with one epic Postmates delivery prior to his set at Scott’s inaugural Astroworld music festival in Houston on Saturday, November 17.

Likely aware that Malone is a huge fan of Postmates – the “Better Now” singer was named the delivery app’s most dedicated customer after spending more than $40,000 in a period of about 400 days – Scott, 26, enlisted a quartet of Houston Rockets cheerleaders to surprise Malone, 23, in his dressing room with his preferred Postmates order.

The hand-delivered surprise consisted of several trays of well done Chick-fil-A chicken tenders with 160 ounces of buffalo sauce and six gallons of sweet tea, and clearly took the food fanatic by surprise. “I love you buddy!” Malone told Scott upon seeing his special treat. “Thank you so much.”

The “Congratulations” singer then proceeded to thank each of the cheerleaders before declaring he couldn’t wait to dig into his feast. “I’m ready to get a little f–ed up,” Malone said with a smile. “Let’s get it on.”

In addition to his obsession with Postmates, Post Malone made his feelings about food clear during a televised trip to Olive Garden with Jimmy Fallon back in October. During Fallon’s first trip to the restaurant chain, Malone taught him how to properly eat a breadstick – smothered in butter – and opened the host’s eyes to the wonders of all the free food Olive Garden has to offer. Exclaimed Fallon: “You don’t pay for anything at this place. It’s unbelievable!”

And while Malone got a thoughtful surprise from Scott, the “Sicko Mode” rapper got a sweet surprise of his own when his 9-month-old daughter, Stormi Webster, said “Dada” for the first time on Sunday, November 18. “Trying to teach her some new words,” mom Kylie Jenner captioned the cute Instagram video, which also featured the reality star attempting to get her little one to say “Kylie Cosmetics.”
