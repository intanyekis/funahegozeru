Kehlani, Fashion Nova Donate $50,000 To LGBTQ Centers

published on June 13, 2024
R&B singer Kehlani continues to be a champion of the LGBTQ community, and thanks to her and her partnership with the online clothing boutique Fashion Nova, the “Honey” singer was able to donate $50,000 to LGBTQ centers across the country. “THAT is what i’m most proud of this year,” wrote the songbird on her Twitter page. Among the centers that she has donated to include the Reclaim Center, which provides mental health care for young people who identify as queer or trans, and the Ruth Ellis Center, a service based in Detroit that aims to help runaway, homeless, and at-risk LGBTQ youth.
