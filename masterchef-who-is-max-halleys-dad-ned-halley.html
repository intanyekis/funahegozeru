Max's Sandwich Shop

Max opened his famous Max’s Sandwich Shop in 2014 on Stroud Green Road in north London. The shop is famous for sandwiches as it is really good.

The shop's signature ham, egg ‘n’ chips creation on home-baked focaccia is what people seem to love a lot.

Max's idea for the shop came alive when he realized that the only real sandwiches that people can get were from Pret, caffs, and salt beef bagels on Brick Lane.  Similarly, he pinpointed the truth that no one was making sandwiches well as a proper meal, so he gave a try to make a proper meal.

Similarly, he was asked during an interview in Drakes how he convinced people that sandwiches were something they could have for dinner. He replied that it was simple as he only opened in the evenings.

Besides being a chef and shop owner, he is also a celebrity chef as he appeared in a cooking show on Channel 4. He is also seen on Sunday Brunch. Similarly, he was also seen as a guest chef on ITV's This Morning.

The chef has also done large-scale media and social media campaigns for brands like Tabasco, Gaviscon, Oatly, Branston Pickle, and Uk Tourist Board.

He was also seen making a series called The Sandwich Show for VICE/Munchies in 2017. In the series, he was seen traveling to the UK to find inspiration for a new sandwich to put on his menu.

He is not only a chef but also an author and has written several books. He has written books like Max’s Pic-Nic Book and Max’s Sandwich Book. In those books, there are his recipes.

His first book Max’s Sandwich Book, was in the Top Ten Bestseller of Sunday Times. He is also seen writing regularly for the Evening Standard.
