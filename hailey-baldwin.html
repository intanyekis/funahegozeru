Hailey Baldwin is an American model who is widely recognized as the wife of the pop artist, Justin Bieber. She is also a successful media personality and socialite who was trained to become a ballet dancer but all dream flattered after she got heavily injured while dancing. 

Talking about her modeling career, in 2012 she walked at Sherri Hill's Prom and gathered a quite good amount of followers. Her major impression was held at London Fashion Week by walking in the Topshop Unique show. Having said that she has now featured in various top ads and magazines and has worked with brands like Ralph Lauren, Guess, and Tommy Hilfiger.

Engaged with singer, Justin Bieber

Hailey's relationship is as popular as her career. She met  Bieber for the very first time back in 2015. They started their relationship as a friend and later got into a serious relationship. They briefly started dating one another in December 2015 and started appearing in various public places together. 

However, their relationship lasted for a couple of months and the couple split in January 2016. After two years of their separation, they continued their relationship in May 2018 and surprised their fans. In July 2018 the couple gave heartwarming news to their fans about their engagement. Fans from all around the world congratulate the couple.

Walk-in aisle with Justin Bieber

In November 2018 the pair exchanged their wedding vows after two months of their engagement. According to the couple the ceremony was held between close friends and family on 30th September 2019. They announced their wedding to their fans through their respective social media account. Justin even uploaded a photo of his and Hailey holding hands together where he captioned 'My wife is awesome.

Whereas Baldwin changed her Instagram name from @hailybaldwin into @haileybieber. As of now, the pair are now married for more than one year and seems very happy with one another. They often upload one another's pictures on their social media account where the newlywed couple is seen spending quality time together. Recently Justin pranked his fans stating that Hailey is pregnant.
