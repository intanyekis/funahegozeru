Kaun Banega Crorepati 2023 Start Date

The last season of Kaun Banega Crorepati started on Aug 14, 2022, and it concluded on Dec 30, 2022. The 14th season of Kaun Banega Crorepati ran for a total of 5 months. Therefore, it is already known that the show will run for five months this year too. Talking about the pattern of the show, it will also remain the same. 10 contestants will be selected from the thousands of applicants for the Fast as Finger as Fast game.
