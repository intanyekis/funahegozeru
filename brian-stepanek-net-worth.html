Brian Stepanek Wiki Biography

Brian Stepanek was born on the 6th February 197,1 in Cleveland, Ohio USA, and is an actor and voice actor, best known for his roles in such television series as “The Suite Life of Zack and Cody” (2005-2008), “Brian O’Brian” (2008), and “Nicky, Ricky, Dicky & Dawn” (2014- present).

Have you ever wondered how rich Brian Stepanek is, as of mid- 2017? According to authoritative sources, it has been estimated that Stepanek’s net worth is as high as $1 million, an amount earned through his successful acting career which started in the late ‘90s.

Brian Stepanek Net Worth $1 Million

Brian Stepanek grew up in Ohio where he went to the Gilmour Academy from 1985 to 1989, and then attended the Syracuse University in New York.

Brian’s on-screen debut came in 1998 in an episode of the Primetime Emmy Award-nominated series “Early Edition”, and then he appeared in an episode of the romantic-comedy show called “Cupid” (1998). Stepanek’s first feature movie appearance was in the “Children of the Struggle” (1999), in which he played Deputy Assailant. In the early 2000s, Brian had a role in the comedy “Kissing Jessica Stein” (2001), portraying Peter in a story about the girl who is searching for the perfect man, but finds the perfect woman instead. The following year, Stepanek had a minor part in the thriller called “Murder by Numbers” starring Sandra Bullock, Ben Chaplin and Ryan Gosling, while in 2005, he appeared in Michael Bay’s “The Island” alongside Scarlett Johansson, Ewan McGregor and Djimon Hounsou.

From 2005 to 2008, Brian played in the Primetime Emmy Award-nominated show called “The Suite Life of Zack and Cody”, as the character Arwin in 27 episodes of a story about two identical twins and their mother. In the meantime, he appeared as Sector Seven Agent in Michael Bay’s Oscar Award-nominated blockbuster “Transformers” (2007) starring Shia LaBeouf, Megan Fox and Josh Duhamel; the movie made over $700 million worldwide, and also helped increase Stepanek’s net worth significantly. In 2008, he starred in the show “Brian O’Brian”, the story about his hilarious misadventures, while from 2010 to 2011, he leant his voice to the character of Harold Buttowski in the Primetime Emmy Award-nominated series “Kick Buttowski: Suburban Daredevil”.

In 2013, Stepanek teamed up with Michael Bay again in his comedy “Pain & Gain”, with Mark Wahlberg, Dwayne Johnson and Anthony Mackie, while he also appeared in Scott Stewart’s horror called the “Dark Skies”. Since 2014, Brian is playing Tom Harper in a Primetime Emmy Award-nominated series “Nicky, Ricky, Dicky & Dawn”, so far in 84 episodes, while the show is still running. Most recently, he gave his voice to various characters in the animated series “The Loud House” (2016-2017), and at the moment, he is working on the show “Young Sheldon” and also in Rob Reiner’s biography movie “LBJ”, about the former US president Lyndon Baines Johnson, starring Jennifer Jason Leigh, Woody Harrelson and C. Thomas Howell, to be released in November 2017.

Regarding his personal life, Brian Stepanek has been married to Parisa since 2002, and they have three children together.
