Nicole Travolta is an American actress who has an estimated net worth of $1 million. She was born in Los Angeles, California in July 4, 1989. She started her professional career in acting after she played a role in the film Da Vinci’s War in the early 1990's when she was just five years old.

Miss Travolta took some time off from acting to focus on school. She then returned to acting after eight years when she appeared in a short film in 2005. Since then, she has worked on several independent films. She also had guest starring roles in television shows such as One Tree Hill, The Secret Life of the American Teenager, The Middle, It’s You Not Me and Hawaii Five-O.

Nicole is best known for her performance as Eager Beaver on the Cover Up show. The niece of John Travolta, she is set to appear in the films House of Dust and The Pom Pom Massacre.
