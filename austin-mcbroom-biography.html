Austin McBroom Bio (Wiki)

Who is Austin McBroom?

Austin McBroom is a former professional NCAA basketball guard for Saint Louis University.

After his basketball career, Austin McBroom became a social media star and currently has more than 3.7 million followers on Instagram.

Austin McBroom: Age, Parents, Siblings, Ethnicity

Austin McBroom was born in California on May 20, 1992. He was interested in the world of basketball at an early age and hence began playing basketball at the age of 5.

He is of American nationality. His father’s name is Allen McBroom and his mother’s name is Michale McBroom.

Furthermore, there are no details available about his ethnicity at present. He has a brother named Landon.

In which school did Austin McBroom attend?

Talking about his education, McBroom attended Campbell Hall School.

Furthermore, he was enrolled at three colleges namely, Central Michigan University, Saint Louis University, and Eastern Washington University.

Austin McBroom is a major in communication and he had a 3.9 GPA in his graduation degree.

Austin McBroom: Career, Profession

Austin McBroom initially played football and baseball at Campbell Hall School. In addition, he averaged 25 points per game in his senior year.

In the 2013-2014 season, he led the St. Louis Billikens in 3-point shooting makes, and percentages.

After his basketball career from 2011 to 2012, McBroom began his career as a social media star.

Currently, Austin McBroom has over 3.9 M followers on Instagram. Furthermore, he is also a member of the ACE Family collaborative channel.

Austin McBroom: Salary, Net Worth

He has a salary of $600k which he earns from his Youtube channel. Additionally, he has a net worth of $2 million as of 2022.

Austin McBroom: Rumors, Controversy

Austin McBroom became part of a controversy after he was accused of cheating on Catherine in the COACHELLA video. He later addressed the rumor as completely false.

How tall is Austin McBroom?

Talking about his body measurement, Austin McBroom has a height of 5 feet 9 inches or 175 cm. Additionally, he weighs around 74 kg or 163 lbs.

Furthermore, his hair color and eye color are dark brown.
