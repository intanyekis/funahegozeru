Who Is Brad Kalilimoku? Details To Know About His Romantic Life

Brad Kalilimoku is a former University of Hawai’i Football Player turned model and actor known for Finding’ Ohana (2021) and Hawaii Five-0 (2010). He landed a massive role in the Netflix hit film Finding’ Ohana; although his character doesn’t speak much, his character gave a significant meaning.

As an athlete, Brad has good stats on his career in college football. So far, Brad Kalilimoku has worked in Hollywood, and his artwork has been released in English-language movies. He stars as Kua Kawena in Netflix action comedy film Finding’ Ohana.

Who Is Brad Kalilimoku?

Brad Kalilimoku is an American actor well-known for his appearance in Hawaii Five-0 and Finding ‘Ohana.

His parents are Roxanne and Fredrick Kalilimoku, who is from Honolulu, O’ahu. And he grew up with his brother named Chad, who was a former Warrior.

Before pursuing acting, he used to play a college football player at the University of Hawai.

He graduated his high schooling from Roosevelt high school. In his senior year on college football, he played in all 13 games, starting in 11 at linebacker, and led the team with season-high ten tackles versus Northern Colorado.

He had 2.5 sacks for 16 lost yards versus Fresno State and recorded two quarterback hurries and two pass break-ups.
