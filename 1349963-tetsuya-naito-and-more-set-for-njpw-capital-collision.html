Tetsuya Naito And More Set For NJPW Capital Collision

is loading up the cards for their returns to Philadelphia and Washington D.C.

NJPW will hold the Capital Collision event on April 15 and the Collision in Philadelphia event on April 16. A number of the company’s top stars are set to compete at the shows, and NJPW is adding to the lineups.

On NJPW’s official website, the company announced that Los Ingobernables de Japon will be a part of both shows. Tetsuya Naito, IWGP Junior Heavyweight Champion Hiromu Takahashi, and SANADA have been confirmed for the shows, which will mark the first time Okada and SANADA have competed in the United States since 2019. El Desperado of Strong Style has also been confirmed for both events.

Plus, Lance Archer has been announced for the Philadelphia show. This will mark Archer’s first NJPW show since the World Tag League.

WrestleZone will provide more information as it becomes available.
