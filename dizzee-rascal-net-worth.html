Dizzee Rascal is an English rapper, record producer, and songwriter with an estimated net worth of $3.5 million. Most of his wealth was acquired from his music and pretty successful records.

Dizzee Rascal was born in London, England and he has been active member in the music industry since 1999. Dizzee is currently one of the most popular and well-known British rappers whose music could be described as a blend of hip-hop, electronic music, garage, reggae and pop with some eclectic sample and exotic style.

Some of his most famous number one hits are Bonkers, Holiday, Dance Wiv Me, Shout, Dirtee Disco and Bassline Junkie. Dizzee Rascal also earned good portion of his wealth by producing various record labels, mostly from British hip-hop genre for rap artists like Rizzle Kicks.

Dizzee Rascal also made few collaborations with famous music artists like Shakira, Robbie Williams, Florence and the Machine and Armand Van Helden
