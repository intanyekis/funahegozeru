She joined Chicago Actors Studio in 2016 and remained there through 2018. She appeared in Gilley’s Dallas Event venue’s Christmas Show, Halloween Show, Broadway Show, and Art of Love productions. In the movie The Unicorn, where she played the part of Nika, she made her screen debut in 2019.

Madelyn Kientz portrayed the part of Emily in the movie Adventure of Rufus: The Fantastic Pet a year later. She became well-known when she made an appearance in the Big Sky television series, which made its debut on ABC in November 2020. She plays the part of Max in the show, a young woman who wants to get away from her mother’s new partner.
