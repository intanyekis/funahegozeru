Bedurulanka 2012 Movie (2023): Cast | Trailer | Songs | OTT

Bedurulanka 2012 is the next Telugu thriller drama written and directed by Clax. Kartikeya Gummakonda forays into the major role, and Neha Shetty pairs the opposite. The film is produced by Ravindra Banerjee Muppaneni under the Loukya Entertainments banner. Music director Mani Sharma composed the background scores and soundtracks for the film. Sony Music Entertainment India Pvt. Ltd bagged the music label.

From the poster, it is evident that the film takes to a different genre that unravels the mystery and thriller moments.
