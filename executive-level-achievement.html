Executive Level achievement in Starfield

This is the last mission for the faction Ryujin Industries. There are 13 missions for this faction. You’ll also get the ‘Back to the Grind’ achievement for joining Ryujin Industries. Along with ‘Guilty Parties’ achievement which is mission 10 in this series.

To join Ryujin Industries you’ll need to apply for a job at a kiosk, I’m not certain but I don’t think it matters what answers you give in this (based from watching a video vs my playthrough).

Once done you’ll need meet Imogene Salzo for an interview in the Rjujin Tower on Neon, found on Volii Alpha, which is part of the Volii system.

I don’t think the answers in the interview with Imogene, determine whether or not you get the job at Ryujin Industries.
