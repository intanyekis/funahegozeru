Charlie Puth Celebrity Profile Hollywood Life

Bio:
Charlie Puth (born Charles Otto Puth Jr. on December 2, 1991 in Rumson, New Jersey) is an American singer. He got his big break when his song “See You Again” with Wiz Khalifa became a worldwide sensation after the release of Furious 7. He released his debut single “Marvin Gaye” in Feb. 2015, which featured vocals from Meghan Trainor. The two shared a steamy kiss after the performance of the song at the 2015 AMAs on Nov. 22, 2015. Charlie’s debut album Nine Track Mind will be released on Jan. 22, 2016.

Best Known For:
Charlie is best known for singing “See You Again” with Wiz for the Furious 7 soundtrack. The song served as a tribute to Paul Walker, who tragically passed away in Nov. 2013.

Personal Life:

Charlie keeps his personal life private, but he was linked to Selena Gomez in Sept. 2015, who later said they were just friends, and Bella Thorne at the end of 2016 after they were photographed flaunting PDA on the beach. As of Oct. 2017, he is rumored to be dating actress Danielle Campbell. Charlie has a scar on right eye that he got after he was bit by a dog when he was two years old.
