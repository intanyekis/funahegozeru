Bella Hadid Wears Skimpy Leotards in Love Advent Video

The sexy just doesn’t stop! Right after making her runway debut (in very little clothing) at the 2016 Victoria’s Secret Fashion Show in Paris, Bella Hadid is back and barer than ever. The Dior Makeup ambassador, 20, kicks off the first day of Love’s Advent 2016 calendar, showing her flexibility in more ways than one.

Hadid got into formation in a slew of ’80s-inspired leotards and leg warmers accessorized with Fenty Puma by Rihanna sneaker booties, drop earrings, hoops, skinny belts and more. The model, who was actually born in 1996, didn’t miss a beat with her acrobatic moves on a yoga ball and a mat and with mini weights. She even took a break to fuel, eating a banana in slow motion. She served vintage vibes with her glam thanks to sparkly pink and blue eyeshadow and her side-swept and slightly wet brunette locks.

She went for a look at the November 30 fashion show, outfitting her curves in a gray crystal-studded one-piece and floor-sweeping kimono, among other getups, as ex-boyfriend and performer The Weeknd looked on.
