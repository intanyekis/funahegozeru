The Chainsmokers' Alex Pall's Dating History

The Chainsmokers’ Alex Pall has been in the spotlight for more than a decade — and so has his love life.

The “Closer” artist, who heads the EDM duo with Drew Taggart, hasn’t exactly been shy about his dating pursuits, candidly discussing his NSFW priorities, revealing he and Taggart had threesomes with fans and openly mourning the loss of a past relationship after he was publicly caught cheating.

In January 2023, the Chainsmokers revealed on the “Call Her Daddy” podcast that they’ve had threesomes with their fans.

“Does it count if it’s international?” Pall quipped, explaining that their ménage à trois “were never planned.” Still, the EDM artist admitted that there was some awkwardness about the situation. “It’s weird, I’m not gonna lie.”
