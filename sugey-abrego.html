Sugey Abrego is a famous Mexican model, actress, and TV presenter. She is recognized for being a weather girl for a Mexican TV station Canal 4. Sugey is also known for being a host of Arriesga TV and appearing in Mexican telenovelas such as Barrera de Amor, Destilando Amor, La Rosa de Guadalupe, and others. Apart from her professional career, Sugey has gained a massive online following through her social media. Her Instagram account alone had 315,000 followers at one point, and the number keeps rising.
