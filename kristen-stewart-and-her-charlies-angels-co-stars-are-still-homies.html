Kristen Stewart And Her Charlie's Angels Co-Stars Are 'Still Homies'

The trio of 'Charlie's Angels' is as close onscreen as it is in real life, as actress Ella Balinska confirmed.

In a recent interview with 'Harper's Bazaar', the British actress looked back on her experience on the set of the movie also starring Kristen Stewart and Naomi Scott. The movie, directed by Elizabeth Banks, marked Balinska's film debut.

Ella Balinska Said Her Friendship With Kristen Stewart And Naomi Scott Is 'Special'

Despite receiving mixed reviews, the reboot also gave her the chance to establish a close relationship with Scott and Stewart. The 'Spencer' star had invited Balinska to the premiere of the film where she portrays Princess Diana.

"Sadly, I was out the country, but we’re all still homies," Balinska told Harper's Bazaar.

"It’s a special friendship and a good one to have in this crazy industry we’ve all found ourselves in," she added.

After that role, Balinska went to star in horror movie 'Run Sweetheart Run' and Netflix's 'Resident Evil' and explained how physical roles became her thing.

"I’ve just been riding the wave of playing very physical characters, not only because I can do it, but also because I connect with them," the actress said.

"It’s fun to know that you have something that you can do well," she added.

Will Balinska Attend Kristen Stewart's Wedding?

Given what Balinska said, it wouldn't be unlikely for her and Scott to be invited to Stewart's wedding.

Whether it will be celebrated by Guy Fieri or not, Stewart is marrying her girlfriend Dylan Meyer, as she previously announced.

The actress and the Moxie screenwriter are officially getting married after two years together. Stewart gushed over the proposal in a recent interview, saying that her partner nailed it.

"We're marrying, we're totally gonna do it," Stewart said on SiriusXM's 'The Howard Stern Show' in November.

"I wanted to be proposed to, so I think I very distinctly carved out what I wanted and she nailed it. We're marrying, it's happening," she also added.

The Twilight star has never made it a secret that she wanted to walk down the aisle with her girlfriend.

Over a year ago, Stewart said she couldn't wait to get married to Meyer.

"I think good things happen fast," she told Stern at the time.

In fact, she even revealed she had an elaborate proposal already planned, though she didn't divulge the details in case Meyer was listening.

"I have a couple plans that are, like, just the coolest things to do. It's pretty undeniable," she shared on The Howard Stern Show in 2019.

But it looks like Meyer beat her to it and was the one who proposed instead.
