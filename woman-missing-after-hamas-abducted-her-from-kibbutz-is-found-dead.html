Celeste Fishbein Death; Woman Missing After Hamas Abducted Her From Kibbutz Is Found Dead

An 18-year-old Israeli lady who was kidnapped by Hamas from an Israeli kibbutz and has been missing since Oct. 7 was found dead, her uncle stated over the phone on Tuesday. Celeste Fishbein resided in Kibbutz Be’eri, near the Gaza border, where more than 100 people were killed in the Hamas onslaught. May their souls rest in peace.

“A woman from the Israeli army, who has the hardest job in the world, came to our home today to inform us of Celeste’s death,” her uncle, Mario Fishbein, said via phone from Israel.

Celeste was seized from her home on the day of the invasion and slain on the way to Gaza, according to her uncle, who did not know the details of her death. Celeste, whose mother is Brazilian, worked as a babysitter and was planning to apply for Brazilian citizenship, according to her uncle.
