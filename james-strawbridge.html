James Strawbridge- Biography

James Strawbridge is well-known for his television appearances as a celebrity chef and a leading advocate of sustainable living. Strawbridge rose to stardom after appearing in The Re-Inventors alongside his father, Dick Strawbridge. In addition, he and his father appeared in the show Hungry Sailors Duo.

James Strawbridge- Birth, Age, Ethnicity, Siblings, Education
